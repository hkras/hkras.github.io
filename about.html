HANNA KRAS

RESUME ABOUT

Hanna Kras is a front-end web developer, writer, videographer, and overall communicator, with experience in public, private, and voluntary sectors.

She got her start in the wild west days of the internet, altering markup to get the perfect color scheme and look and feel for her Xanga (RIP), then her MySpace. Later she partnered with an online friend to create a website to host their StarCraft clan and run a couple of bots in their channel. What began as a hobby soon turned into a career when she discovered her passions for English, communications, film, and design could be paired with coding to create her own brand new webpages and interfaces from scratch.

Hanna works as an Internet Architect for the Fairfax County Park Authority, where she designs and creates webpages with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript; designs and delivers marketing emails; develops web forms; and ensures content is accessible, mobile responsive, and user friendly. Her keen eye for details and her critical-thinking skills enabled her to streamline the update process on dozens of webpages through development of custom components.

As a lifelong lover of learning, Hanna is always looking to add new tools to her toolbox with continued education in front-end development, JavaScript/jQuery, UI/UX design, interaction design, graphic design, Python, SQL, and computer science.
